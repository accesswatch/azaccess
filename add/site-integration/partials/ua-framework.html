Digital Accessibility Framework

Use this overview to introduce the four framework areas: Understand, Prepare, Build, Maintain.

Understand

Build foundational knowledge of digital accessibility, including key concepts, principles and the reasons behind them.

Prepare

Consider accessibility elements in the design of resources, while emphasizing best practices and standards.

Build

Create accessible products and environments, like documents, slides, PDFs, spreadsheets, and websites.

Maintain

Sustain and enhance accessibility compliance over time, including developing strategies, conducting audits, and iterative updates.
