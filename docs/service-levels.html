Overview

The Digital Accessibility team provides services to help units across the university achieve and maintain accessibility compliance. This document outlines our service level commitments.

Service Hours

  • Standard support:Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM (Arizona Time)
  • Emergency support:Available for critical accessibility barriers
  • Holidays:University holidays observed

Priority Definitions

Priority Definition Examples
Critical Complete barrier to access; individual cannot complete essential task Registration blocked, form inaccessible, no alternative available
High Significant barrier; workaround is difficult or unavailable Major navigation issues, missing captions for required video
Medium Barrier exists; workaround available Poor contrast, missing headings, non-descriptive links
Low Minor issue; minimal impact Best practice violations, minor inconsistencies

Feedback and Complaints

Response times for accessibility feedback and complaints submitted through official channels.

Response Commitments

Priority Initial Response Resolution Target Interim Accommodation
Critical 4 hours 24–48 hours Immediate (if fix delayed)
High 1 business day 5 business days Within 24 hours
Medium 2 business days 15 business days As needed
Low 3 business days 30 business days N/A

What to Expect

  1. Acknowledgment:Confirmation your feedback was received
  2. Assignment:Issue assigned to appropriate team
  3. Updates:Regular status updates until resolved
  4. Resolution:Notification when fix is complete
  5. Follow-up:Confirmation the fix works for you

Escalation Path

If response times are not met or you're unsatisfied with the resolution:

  1. Reply to your original feedback ticket
  2. Contact the Digital Accessibility Director
  3. Contact the Disability Resource Center
  4. File a formal complaint with Equal Opportunity

Consultation Services

Expert guidance on accessibility implementation.

Consultation Types

What's Included

Testing Services

Accessibility testing and auditing services.

Testing Options

Service Turnaround Deliverable
Quick check (single page) 1 business day Summary email
Focused audit (up to 10 pages) 5 business days Issue list with recommendations
Full site audit (sample-based) 10-15 business days Full report with WCAG mapping
Application audit 15-20 business days Full report with WCAG mapping
Pre-launch review 3 business days Go/no-go assessment

Testing Methodology

  • Automated scanning:Using industry tools (axe, WAVE, SiteImprove)
  • Manual testing:Keyboard navigation, screen reader testing, visual review
  • WCAG evaluation:Systematic check against WCAG 2.1 AA criteria
  • User testing:Available for major projects (by arrangement)

Report Contents

  • Executive summary
  • Testing scope and methodology
  • Issue list with severity ratings
  • WCAG success criteria mapping
  • Remediation recommendations
  • Retesting guidance
