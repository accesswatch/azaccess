YuJa Panorama for Students

Transform your course content into formats that match how you learn best.

Why Panorama?

Have you ever read a paragraph over and over trying to understand what you're reading? Are there times when you would like to listen to your course materials while exercising or commuting?

YuJa Panorama is a learning tool in Brightspace (D2L) that easily transforms your course content into a format that meets your learning needs in a way that works for you.

Getting started

  1. Find the alternative formats icon – Look for the person-inside-a-circle icon next to each course file in Brightspace.

    The icon appears next to documents, PDFs, and other course materials.

  2. Choose your format – Select the format that works best for you and download.

Format options

Choose from these accessible formats for your course materials:

Reading formats

Source File
View the document in its original format, suitable for download viewing.
PDF
View the document in PDF format, suitable for both online and download viewing.
Text File
View the document in pure text format without any formatting styles, suitable for both online and download viewing.
Enhanced HTML
HTML documents displayed in a dedicated tab with customizable appearance using the Website Accessibility tool.
EPUB
View the document in ebook format, suitable for any ebook reader application.
Gradient Reader
Enhances visual word recognition when reading by providing a guiding color gradient.
Immersive Reader
Allows customizations to text preferences, grammar options, and reading preferences while following along with audio playback.

Audio formats

Audio Podcast
Audio playback for documents, suitable for both online and download listening. Great for commuting or exercising.

Accessibility formats

Braille
View the document in braille grade 2 format, suitable for download viewing (a braille reader is required).
OCR (Optical Character Recognition)
Convert images with text into machine-encoded text that can be searched and read by screen readers.

Math and science formats

LaTeX
Convert documents containing math equations into LaTeX document format.
Math Text
Convert math documents into printed English, including numbers and symbols written as words.
Math Audio
Convert math documents to MP3 audio files.
MathML
Extract mathematical equations from documents and convert them into accessible HTML files.

Text-to-speech with SmartSpeaker

YuJa Panorama also provides text-to-speech options for listening to your course content and quizzes inside Brightspace.

Learn how to use SmartSpeaker

Need more help?

Panorama may meet most alternative format requests. However, if you need textbooks or other academic materials in alternate formats as a disability-related accommodation, connect with the Disability Resource Center.

For general questions about YuJa Panorama, contact accessibility@arizona.edu.
