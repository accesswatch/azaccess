Teaching & LearningUpdated

Support inclusive courses across Brightspace, Panopto, Zoom, and classroom experiences.

Instructor response goal: 2 business days for accommodation letters

Owner: UCATT Teaching & Learning + DRC · Last reviewed: 2026-01-05 · Next review: 2026-04-05

Executive summary

Students must receive accessible materials and timely accommodations. Align your syllabus, LMS content, media, and assessments with UCATT and DRC guidance.

  • Use Brightspace templates and theLMS checklist.
  • Caption Panopto/Zoom recordings within 3 days and provide transcripts.
  • Respond to DRC accommodation letters promptly, documenting adjustments.
  • Offer multiple ways to engage, including accessible labs and group work.

If you only do one thing:post slides or notes before class and caption your next recorded lecture.

Quick wins (5–10 minutes each)

Four high-impact actions you can do today. Each links to guidance from the source.

Share slides before class

Post your slides to D2L before the lecture. Students can follow along, take notes, and review later.

Pedagogy checklist

Media workflow

Follow UCATT’s decision tree for captions, transcripts, and audio description.

  • Panopto: enable machine captions, edit for accuracy, request professional captions for high-stakes content.
  • Zoom: enable live captions and save transcripts for asynchronous review.
  • YouTube/VoiceThread: upload captions or use third-party caption files.

Reference theMedia & Captioning guide.

Inclusive pedagogy playbooks

STEM labs

Provide tactile diagrams or 3D prints, describe visuals verbally, and pair students intentionally to support note taking and lab navigation.

Writing & discussion-heavy courses

Offer collaborative documents with heading styles, ensure comment text has sufficient contrast, and provide clear deadlines/time estimates.

Experiential & field learning

Share itineraries early, confirm transportation accessibility, and plan for interpreters or captioners in mobile settings.
