Practical best practices

  • Captions & transcripts: Provide accurate captions and downloadable transcripts; audit auto-captions.
  • Keyboard accessibility: Ensure controls work by keyboard and focus is visible.
  • Semantic structure: Use headings and native text; avoid images of text.
  • Alt text: Add meaningful alt text; include long descriptions for complex visuals.
  • Color & contrast: Verify WCAG AA contrast ratios.
  • Alternatives for complex interactions: Provide text-based backups for drag-and-drop etc.
  • Testing: Test with NVDA/VoiceOver and keyboard-only navigation across browsers.
