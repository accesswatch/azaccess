Accessibility Governance Charter

Updated: 2025-11-20 · Next review: 2026-05-20

Purpose

This charter describes how the University of Arizona coordinates digital accessibility work across colleges, departments, and partner units to meet WCAG 2.2 AA and Title II expectations.

Roles

  • Executive Sponsor: authorizes budget, resolves escalations, and signs external commitments.
  • Accessibility Program Office: manages roadmap, content standards, and crosswalk mapping.
  • Partner Units: DRC, UCATT, Arizona Digital, Procurement, Equity, Communications, and others implement and maintain accessible experiences.

Cadence

  • Quarterly steering meeting with KPIs, risk review, and decision log.
  • Monthly shared content and registry review.
  • Annual Title II readiness assessment and roadmap refresh.
