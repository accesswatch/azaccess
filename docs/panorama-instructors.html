YuJa Panorama for Instructors

How Panorama helps make your course materials accessible to all students.

What Panorama does for your courses

YuJa Panorama is integrated into Brightspace and automatically provides alternative format options for course documents you upload. Students can access your materials in formats that work best for their learning needs—without you having to create multiple versions.

How it works

  1. Upload your materials – Add documents, PDFs, and other files to your Brightspace course as usual.
  2. Panorama processes them – The system automatically generates alternative formats.
  3. Students choose their format – They click the accessibility icon next to files to download in their preferred format.

You don't need to do anything extra—Panorama handles the conversion automatically.

Formats available to students

Panorama can convert your course materials into:

  • Audio Podcast – Students can listen while commuting or exercising
  • Enhanced HTML – Mobile-friendly web version
  • Selectable Text PDF – Searchable, highlightable PDFs from scanned documents
  • EPUB – For eReaders and tablets
  • Gradient Reader – Color gradients that aid reading
  • Immersive Reader – Customizable text with audio
  • Braille – For students using braille readers
  • Math Formats – LaTeX, Math Text, Math Audio, MathML for STEM content

Best practices for source documents

Panorama works best when your source documents are already well-structured:

  • Use real text – Avoid images of text when possible
  • Use heading styles – Structure documents with Heading 1, Heading 2, etc.
  • Add alt text to images – Describe images for students who can't see them
  • Use readable fonts – Standard fonts convert more reliably
  • Provide source files when possible – Word documents convert better than scanned PDFs

See our document accessibility guides for detailed instructions.

SmartSpeaker for quizzes

Panorama's SmartSpeaker feature provides text-to-speech for quiz content in Brightspace, helping students who benefit from audio support during assessments.

Learn about SmartSpeaker

When students need more

Panorama handles most alternative format needs automatically. For accommodations beyond what Panorama provides, students should work with the Disability Resource Center.

If you receive an accommodation letter for a student requiring specific formats, the DRC's Strategic Alternative Format (SAF) team can help with materials Panorama can't convert.

Questions?

Contact accessibility@arizona.edu for questions about Panorama or making your course materials accessible.
