Panopto Accessibility Guide

Make your Panopto recordings accessible with captions, transcripts, and navigation.

Reading time: 8 minutes

Panopto Accessibility Features

Panopto includes built-in features for accessibility:

  • Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)— Auto-generates captions
  • Caption editing— Correct and improve auto-captions
  • Searchable transcripts— Users can search within video content
  • Keyboard navigation— Full keyboard support
  • Screen reader support— Compatible with major screen readers
  • Adjustable playback speed— Viewers can slow down or speed up

Adding & Editing Captions

Enable Automatic Captions

Auto-captions generate automatically, but you can check the setting:

  1. Open your video in Panopto
  2. ClickSettings(gear icon)
  3. Go toCaptions
  4. Ensure "Automatic captions" is enabled

Processing time: About 1x the video length (a 30-minute video takes ~30 minutes to caption).

Editing Captions (Essential!)

Auto-captions are typically 70-80% accurate. Youmust edit themfor compliance.

  1. Open the video in the Panopto editor
  2. ClickCaptionsin the left panel
  3. Click any caption segment to edit
  4. Make corrections—technical terms and names need special attention
  5. Use keyboard shortcuts:
    • Tab— Move to next caption
    • Shift+Tab— Move to previous caption
    • Enter— Save and move to next
  6. ClickApplywhen done

Caption Accuracy Requirement

Aim for99% accuracy. Auto-captions alone don't meet accessibility requirements. Always review and correct them.

Uploading Captions

If you have caption files from another source:

  1. Open the video Settings Captions
  2. ClickImport captions
  3. Upload .srt or .vtt file
  4. Choose language and apply

Caption Best Practices

  • Speaker identification:Add [Professor:] or [Student:] for multiple speakers
  • Sound descriptions:Add [music playing], [applause], [pause] as needed
  • Technical terms:Double-check spelling of jargon and proper nouns
  • Timing:Ensure captions don't fall behind the audio

Transcripts

Panopto automatically generates a searchable transcript from captions.

Viewing Transcripts

  • Click theTranscripticon in the video player
  • Transcript appears in a side panel
  • Click any line to jump to that point in the video

Downloading Transcripts

  1. Open video Settings Captions
  2. ClickDownload captions
  3. Choose format: .srt, .vtt, or plain text

Recording for Better Accessibility

Improve caption quality and viewer experience by following these tips when recording:

Audio Quality

  • Use a good microphone (external USB mic recommended)
  • Record in a quiet environment
  • Speak clearly and at moderate pace
  • Minimize background noise

Speaking Tips

  • Spell out unusual names and terms the first time
  • Pause briefly between topics
  • Avoid filler words (um, uh) that clutter captions
  • Describe what you're showing on screen

Visual Content

  • Verbally describe important visual content
  • Read text shown on slides aloud
  • Describe diagrams and images
  • Don't say "as you can see" without describing what's visible

Accessibility Settings

Viewer Controls

Students can customize their viewing experience:

  • Playback speed:0.5x to 2x speed
  • Caption toggle:Turn captions on/off
  • Caption appearance:Some browsers allow caption styling
  • Full screen:Enlarges video and captions

Keyboard Shortcuts (Viewer)

Key Action
SpaceorK Play/Pause
/ Seek back/forward 5 seconds
J/L Seek back/forward 10 seconds
M Mute/Unmute
F Toggle fullscreen
C Toggle captions

Embedding in Brightspace

  1. In Panopto, clickShareon your video
  2. Copy theEmbed code
  3. In Brightspace, create or edit content
  4. In the HTML editor, clickInsert Stuff Enter Embed Code
  5. Paste the embed code

Embedded videos maintain all accessibility features including captions and transcripts.
