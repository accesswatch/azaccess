How can we help?

Need a review, have a question, or hit a barrier? We're here to help—no expertise required.

We typically respond within 2 business days

Our promise to you

You don't need to know the right terms or rules before reaching out. Just describe what you're working on or what's not working, and we'll take it from there.

Our team coordinates with Arizona Digital, the DRC, UCATT, Procurement, and other campus partners to get you answers quickly.

What do you need?

Pick the option that best fits, or just email us if you're not sure.

Not sure which to pick?
  • Students: Start with the I am a Student guide or submit an Access Report if you've hit a barrier.
  • Faculty: Check out Teaching & Learning for courses, or request a consultation for reviews.
  • Staff: Use Procurement when buying tools, or request a review for websites, apps, or documents.
