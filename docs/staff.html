Impactful principles & tools

Procurement, events, and departmental sites stay compliant when you weave WCAG 2.2 AA into every workflow. Reference theWCAG 2.2 highlightsplus these toolkits.

Procurement checkpoints
  • Require VPATs that cite WCAG 2.2 AA success criteria (focus appearance, target size, accessible authentication).
  • Ask vendors to demo Accessibility Insights or axe scans and provide DubBot-compatible URLs for monitoring.

Document interim accommodations when criteria fail.

Departmental sites & Quickstart pages
  • RunDubBotweekly andWAVEbefore publishing campaign pages.
  • Use Accessibility Insights FastPass for high-traffic forms or micro-sites.
  • Log results in your unit’s remediation tracker so leadership sees progress.
  • Review link text with theMeaningful Links guideso navigation and CTAs stay descriptive.
Documents, PDFs, and email attachments

Before distributing HR forms, finance packets, or newsletters:

  • Run the Microsoft Accessibility Checker in Word/Excel/PowerPoint.
  • UseAdobe PDF remediationto confirm reading order, tags, and form labels.
  • ApplyWebAIM alt text principlesfor charts and infographics.

Guidance

Procurement workflow
  1. Follow theAccessible Procurement Steps.
  2. Attach VPATs and demo recordings showing AT compatibility (JAWS, NVDA, ZoomText).
  3. Document remediation commitments and interim accommodations before purchasing.
Event planning

Use theInclusive Events checklistto secure ASL/CART, accessible seating, and clear signage. Provide materials in accessible formats 48 hours prior.

Departmental sites

Leverage Quickstart’s accessible components, run Lighthouse/axe scans quarterly, and request consultations for complex widgets or vendor embeds.

  • Spot-check pages withWebAIM WAVEto catch heading, contrast, and alt-text issues quickly.
  • Use Deque’saxe DevTools guidancefor detailed issue paths and remediation tips.

Assistive technologies to consider

Administrative workflows (HR, finance, student services) must support community members using:

  • JAWSandNVDAfor Windows-based enterprise forms.
  • VoiceOverandTalkBackfor mobile-responsive sites and kiosk experiences.
  • FusionandZoomTextfor magnification in ticketing, CRM, and financial systems.

Consult theAssistive Technology Coveragedoc before deploying or renewing tools.
