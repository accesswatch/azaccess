YuJa Panorama

Accessible alternative formats for course content in Brightspace.

What is Panorama?

YuJa Panorama is a learning tool integrated into Brightspace (D2L) that transforms course content into accessible formats. It helps students access materials in ways that work best for their learning needs.

Available formats

Panorama can convert course documents into multiple accessible formats:

  • Audio Podcast – Listen to course content while commuting or exercising
  • Enhanced HTML – Web version optimized for mobile devices
  • Selectable Text PDF – Convert scanned PDFs into searchable, highlightable text
  • EPUB – Read on eReaders and tablets
  • Gradient Reader – Color gradients that aid reading speed and focus
  • Immersive Reader – Customizable text with audio playback
  • Braille – Grade 2 braille format (requires braille reader)
  • Math Formats – LaTeX, Math Text, Math Audio, and MathML for STEM content
