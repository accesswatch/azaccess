Urgent Access Issues

Immediate Response Required

If someone cannot access content or services they need right now:

  1. Email:accessibility@arizona.eduwith "URGENT" in subject
  2. Include:What's inaccessible, who needs access, when it's needed
  3. Response time:Same business day for urgent issues

While waiting for a response, consider:

  • Can you provide the content in an alternative format?
  • Is there a workaround (phone call, in-person assistance)?
  • Can a colleague access and share the information?
