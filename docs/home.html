Quick wins you can do right now

Each of these takes 5 minutes or less and makes a real difference.

Check your document

Open Word or PowerPoint, go toReview Check Accessibility, and fix the top issues before sharing.

2–5 min Learn more

Test with your keyboard

Put your mouse aside. Can you Tab through your page and use Enter to activate buttons? If not, there's work to do.

3 min Learn more

Caption your next video

Upload to Panopto and review the auto-captions. Fix names, jargon, and anything the AI got wrong.

5 min per 10 min video Learn more

Add alt text to images

Right-click any image in your doc or on your site. Add a short description of what the image shows.

30 sec per image Learn more

Share slides before class

Post your slides to D2L before the lecture so students can follow along or review later.

1 min Learn more
