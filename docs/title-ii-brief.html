Title II Web & Mobile Brief

Updated: 2025-11-20 · Next review: 2026-02-20

Key points

  • DOJ requires public entities to conform public-facing web and mobile to WCAG 2.1 AA; UA is aligning to 2.2 AA.
  • Compliance deadline: April 24, 2026 for UA as a large entity.
  • Mobile apps are explicitly in scope alongside websites and conventional electronic documents.

UA expectations

  1. Inventory and prioritize critical services (admissions, financial aid, LMS, HR, public information).
  2. Remediate gaps on top sites and apps by mid-2026.
  3. Document exceptions and alternative access plans.
  4. Maintain compliance through governance, testing, and training.

Executive call to action

Review current state versus KPIs, allocate budget for remediation and training, and require accessibility checkpoints in all digital initiatives.
