Inclusive Pedagogy Checklist

Checklist

  • Share syllabus statements with clear accommodation processes and timelines.
  • Provide multiple means of engagement, including captions, transcripts, and readable slides.
  • Post materials in Brightspace at least 48 hours before class when possible.
  • Offer alternative participation methods (chat, discussion boards, polling).
  • Design assessments to support extended time and flexible formats.
  • Vet third-party tools for accessibility and document alternatives.
  • Gather mid-term feedback on accessibility and adjust as needed.
