Universal Design for Learning (UDL)

UDL is a framework that proactively addresses learner variability. Instead of retrofitting accommodations, design courses that work for everyone from the start.

Three UDL Principles

  • Multiple Means of Engagement: Offer choices in how students engage with content
  • Multiple Means of Representation: Present information in different formats
  • Multiple Means of Action & Expression: Allow varied ways to demonstrate learning
