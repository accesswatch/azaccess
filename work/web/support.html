Support & Consultations

Get help with documents, websites, procurement, events, and assistive technology.

Response target: within 2 business days

Service promise

The Accessibility Program Office coordinates requests with Arizona Digital, DRC, UCATT, Procurement, and partner teams. Submit a request and we will respond within two business days with next steps.

You don't need perfect language or to know the rules before reaching out—plain-language descriptions of what you're seeing are enough.

How can we help?

Use this quick map to choose the right form, or try our interactive decision guide or accessibility wizard to find exactly what you need.

Not sure which option to pick?

  • Students: Start with the Student guide or submit an Access Report if you hit a barrier.
  • Faculty and instructors: Use the Teaching & Learning hub for courses and the consultation form if you need reviews or media support.
  • Staff and units: Use the Procurement & Vendors hub when buying or renewing tools, or request a web, app, or document review through the consultation form.

Feedback
