Support & Consultations

Get help with documents, websites, procurement, events, and assistive technology.

Response target: within 2 business days

Service promise

The Accessibility Program Office coordinates requests with Arizona Digital, DRC, UCATT, Procurement, and partner teams. Submit a request and we will respond within two business days with next steps.

You don't need perfect language or to know the rules before reaching out—plain-language descriptions of what you're seeing are enough.

Feedback

After your consultation, complete the follow-up survey to help us improve. Email accessibility@arizona.edu for urgent questions.

Students will not get in trouble for reporting barriers or asking questions—it simply helps us fix what's not working.
