Accessible Syllabus Example

This example shows a syllabus structured with real headings, lists, and descriptive links.

Spot the difference

  • Headings follow a clear outline instead of using bold, large text at random.
  • Links describe their destination instead of saying “click here.”
  • The accommodations statement is easy to find and written in plain language.

We are not aiming for perfection—just something your future self (and your students) will thank you for.
