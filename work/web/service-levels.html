Accessibility Service Levels

Demo summary of the shared service-levels guidance.

Service level targets

Service Owner Initial response Typical resolution Notes
IT Accessibility consultation Digital Accessibility Team 2 business days Plan within 5 business days; audits within 15 business days Rush requests triaged based on risk and deadlines.
Captioning / media support UCATT Multimedia 2 business days Machine captions within 3 days; professional 5–7 days Coordinate with DRC for ASL/CART.
Procurement accessibility review Procurement + Digital Accessibility 3 business days VPAT feedback within 10 business days Complex systems may require more time.
Barrier report follow-up DRC + Facilities + Digital Accessibility 2 business days Mitigation plan within 10 business days Covers both physical and digital barriers.
