How to use these guides

Each persona provides an executive overview plus drill-down guidance, assistive technology expectations, and UA-specific workflows. Start with the card that matches your primary responsibilities; you can always explore others.

Persona pattern: Every persona page begins with a short "What this page helps you do" section that tells you the page's purpose, and ends with a concise "Next steps" list so you know concrete actions to take this week, this month, and ongoing.

Most people identify with more than one persona over time. Pick the one that best fits the work you are doing today.

Not sure which persona to open? Try the Accessibility Wizard for quick recommendations, or share the visitor guide with families, alumni, and guests.

Need help choosing? Request a consultation.
