## Roles
- **Executive Sponsor (CIO/Provost designee)**: Sets strategic direction, removes roadblocks, approves funding.
- **Accessibility Program Office**: Maintains roadmap, KPIs, shared content registry, and change log.
- **Digital Accessibility Team**: Provides consultations, audits, remediation guidance.
- **Disability Resource Center**: Oversees accommodations, event access, barrier reports.
- **UCATT**: Integrates accessibility into teaching/learning services and LMS tooling.
- **Procurement & Legal**: Enforces accessible purchasing policies and contract language.
- **Arizona Digital**: Manages CMS patterns, design system, training.

## Cadence
- **Quarterly Steering Committee**: Review KPIs, risk register, policy updates.
- **Monthly Operations Standup**: Sync on consultations, procurement reviews, training needs.
- **Annual Strategy Review**: Align with Title II milestones, budget planning, and WCAG updates.

## Deliverables
- Roadmap updates, KPI dashboard, external resource registry, change log, annual report summarizing progress and next-year goals.
