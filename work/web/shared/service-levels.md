| Service                          | Owner                                    | Initial Response | Typical Resolution                                                                | Notes                                                     |
| -------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| IT Accessibility consultation    | Digital Accessibility Team               | 2 business days  | Plan within 5 business days; audits scheduled within 15 business days             | Rush requests triaged based on risk/compliance deadlines. |
| Captioning / media support       | UCATT Multimedia                         | 2 business days  | Machine captions within 3 business days; professional captions 5–7 business days  | Coordinate with DRC for ASL/CART when required.           |
| Procurement accessibility review | Procurement + Digital Accessibility      | 3 business days  | VPAT review feedback within 10 business days                                      | Complex enterprise systems may require extended testing.  |
| Student accommodation intake     | DRC                                      | 5 business days  | Accommodation letters issued within 10 business days after complete documentation | Encourage earlier submissions before term start.          |
| Barrier report follow-up         | DRC + Facilities + Digital Accessibility | 2 business days  | Mitigation plan within 10 business days                                           | Includes both physical and digital barriers.              |
| Mobile/app accessibility consult | Arizona Digital Mobile Guild             | 3 business days  | Audit schedule confirmed within 10 business days                                  | Coordinate with release cycles.                           |
